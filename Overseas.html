Overseas

A Survival Fishing Game

Overseas is a top-down survival game where you must manage your health, supplies, and finances while navigating a dangerous ocean.
Meet daily quotas by fishing at limited-time spots, avoid deadly monsters and hazards, and strategically upgrade your gear—all before the deadline hits.
Will you outlast the waves, or be swallowed by them?

Design Rationale

The main inspiration for our game concept came from the simple fact that most of our members enjoyed fishing. It was something nostalgic and memorable for them.

With that, we identified pixel art as the artstyle that resonated with us the most. The retro-vibes aligned with our main concept - Nostalgia!

For this project, I led the asset creation, creating the bulk of assets, created our overall colour palette and designed the UIUX of the game.
Aside from that, I coded the player and created the daily quota and deadline.
