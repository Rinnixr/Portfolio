Komo Valley: Virtual Pet Sim

Relaxing Pixel Pet Collector

Hatch your very own virtual pets and send them on egg-citing adventures in this relaxing and nostalgic idle game! You can feed your Komos, play with them, and even dress them up however you like.
There are hundreds of cute Komos to collect and interesting places to explore, with more being added constantly. With so many collectibles and a beautiful 8-bit retro pixel art style, you're sure to have tons of fun!
Come explore the stunning new world of Komo Valley now!
