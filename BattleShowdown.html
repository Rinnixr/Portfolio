BattleShowdown

A 2D Fast-Paced Multiplayer Platform Shooter by Ethlas

Battle Showdown, developed by Ethlas, redefines mobile gaming with its exhilarating battle arena format, where players dive into various game modes and maps for intense competition.

This action-packed game uniquely leverages interoperable NFTs, allowing gamers to bring a diverse array of beloved characters into battle.

It's a dynamic blend of strategy, skill, and digital collectibles, setting the stage for epic showdowns and ultimate gaming supremacy.

Overview

Developed by the innovative team at Ethlas, Battle Showdown is a groundbreaking venture into the realm of Web3 gaming.

This multiplayer shooter game masterfully blends the excitement of competitive gameplay with the innovative technology of blockchain, establishing a new paradigm in the gaming industry.

With the backing of industry giants like Sequoia Capital and Dragonfly Capital, Battle Showdown represents a significant leap forward in digital entertainment, offering a glimpse into the future of gaming where technology and play converge in exciting new ways.

For this game, I played the role as the studio lead, managing overseas talents to help build the game according to corporates requests.

I managed a small team of 1 other artists and 2 developers.

I doubled up as the art lead - creating assets and the direction for the game.
